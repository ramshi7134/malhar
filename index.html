‘ITHOKKENTH’ MALHAR ACADEMIC ARTS FEST

‘The 2023 academic arts fest is named 'Ithokkenth', which translates to 'What is this?' in Malayalam. The choice of this term signifies a deliberate exploration of social injustices and inequalities, aiming to provoke critical questioning and introspection within the community. Malhar Academic arts, an annual arts fest organized by the students of Malharul uloom Arabic college, is a vibrant celebration that delves into and encourages creativity across contemporary and traditional domains, while also proudly showcasing elements of Muslim tradition and culture. Renowned for its ability to foster skill development, extracurricular engagement, innovative thought processes, and a genuine passion for learning.
